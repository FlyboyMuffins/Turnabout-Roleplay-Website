About the Project

The Turnabout Role-Play project was created to bring the Ace Attorney community the game the tabletop role-play experience they deserve. This game will be updated every so often to add to or even revamp the core rules.

Project Goals

  • Introduce Ace Attorney fans to Tabletop Role-Playing
  • Introduce Tabletop Role-Playing fans to Ace Attorney
  • Provide those who are fans of both the game they deserve

Turnabout Roleplay and website created by Tabletop Nautilus Games
